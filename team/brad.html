R. Bradley Bryant

Chief Financial Officer

Mr. Bryant is a founder of Trinity Lifestyles Management and is Chief Financial Officer developer and director of the financial/accounting model supporting community business management. His 35 years combined experience as a Navy Supply and Contracting Officer, and as a CPA, focused on small business systems development provides a unique and natural foundation for the challenge of establishing the Trinity business model.

The model integrates policies, procedures, training, customized software, outside vendor support, and process improvement controls to cost effectively support industry leading operational performance and generate accurate and timely financial reporting. The model achieves both efficiency and scalability by using state of the art technology to place most transaction processing, payroll and benefits administration, and resident data base management at the community level while providing real time span of community control and oversight to corporate managers.
