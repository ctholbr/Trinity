Jim Chiavetta

Director & Advisor

Mr. Chiavetta is a strategic advisor to Solomon in the areas of Mergers & Acquisitions, ancillary services with an emphasis in medication management, capital formation, and applied technology. Over Jim’s 25-year career he has been involved in 14 major acquisition transactions, both as buyer & seller. Mr. Chiavetta’s strengths include logistics engineering, refinancing and IPO preparation. Solomon is a founder, advisor and investment partner with TabSafe Medical Services, of which Jim is the Chairman and CEO.
