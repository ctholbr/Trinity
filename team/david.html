David Tyler

Acquisitions Advisor

A 15 year real estate veteran, David is a Director of Evergreen Realty, a Solomon affiliate. Most recently, David has been President & Owner of David C. Tyler Realty, Inc. d.b.a. Corporate Real Estate Services (CRES), an Atlanta based firm specializing in commercial office leasing, pre-development, land acquisitions, property sales and consulting. Mr. Tyler has worked with the principles of Solomon for the last 15 years in securing specialized sites, including many of the initial sites at EdenCare Senior living Services.
