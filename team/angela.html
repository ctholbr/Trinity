Angela Hatney

Company Development Officer

Ms. Hatney has over twelve years of direct experience in assisted living including positions with Sunrise Senior Living Services and Atria. While at Atria, Angela was employed as Divisional Human Resources Manager over 28 communities. Angela was also employed as the Development Manager for a Rehabilitation and Therapy Group. Most recently, Angela has played a key operational and marketing role in the corporate offices of Trinity, specializing in marketing, revenue enhancement, branding and human resources.
