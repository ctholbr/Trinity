Abbey Hall Interior Design

Abbey Hall Interior Design, led by Laurie L. Holbrook, ASID, Allied Member, IIDA, has managed over 30 to 60 engagements encompassing approximately $20,000,000 in furniture, fixtures, and equipment installation and work. Without sacrificing function to form, the designers of Abbey Hall enhance the architectural elements of space, incorporating color and design with the feel of a fine home by using sunlight, fountains, plants and the graciousness of antiques and quality fabrics.
