HCP, Inc.

HCP, an S&P 500 company, focuses on properties serving the healthcare industry. Established in 1985, HCP is a publicly traded company (NYSE:HCP) with $13.3 billion in assets under management as of September 30, 2008. HCP's management team, with an array of experience in real estate acquisition, portfolio management and development, actively manages our holdings. HCP's portfolio includes 704 properties located in 43 states and Mexico, as well as mezzanine loans and other debt instruments, distributed among distinct sectors of healthcare including: senior housing, medical office, life science, hospital and skilled nursing.
