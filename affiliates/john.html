John Hopkins University

The Johns Hopkins University/NIC Seniors Housing & Care program is the first in the nation devoted exclusively to the business of seniors housing and care. Launched in October 1999, it is a partnership between NIC and the Johns Hopkins University School of Professional Studies in Business and Education. The Program offers Graduate courses and degrees, Executive Development Program, Business Case Studies and applied research. Mr. Holbrook is a Founding Sponsor of the program and on the Editorial Board of the The Johns Hopkins University/NIC Seniors Housing and Care Journal.
